About Us

We are a team of passionate students who developed SpeakBetter as part of a hackathon project. Our goal is to empower individuals to improve their public speaking skills and boost their confidence.

SpeakBetter was born out of the belief that everyone has the potential to become a great speaker with the right guidance and practice. Our team is dedicated to providing accessible and effective resources to help individuals overcome their speaking challenges.

Join us on this journey to become better speakers and unlock new opportunities in your personal and professional life!
